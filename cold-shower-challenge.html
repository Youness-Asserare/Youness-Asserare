the cold shower challenge:
A very underrated way to get mentally tough, face stress, fear and anxiety

Preview

6 AM, Standing in the shower area, it's dark and Rock music is blasted out at full volume. That's me a few weeks ago, contemplating whether i take that cold shower or not. I felt something moving in my gut,Anxious,and all of my fears and insecurities racing inside my head. My mind was trying so hard to convice me not to touch that shower handle. I ended up doing it, it was super uncomfortable and stressing but i felt so great and empowered after i was done.



WHAT IS THE COLD SHOWER CHALLENGE ?

Cold shower challenge is simply challenging yourself to take Cold showers DAILY and consistently.
I personally prefer taking it for 5 minutes at least because it's hard.
And btw ! The colder, the better.



WHY AM I DOING IT ?

-For me, it's so easy to get comfortable in my life, and comfort to me means depression,deterioration and sadness.
-If i can't handle a cold shower for 5 minutes, how am i supposed to be ready to face life and its challenges
-Because it's FUN and adrenaline inducing.
-To get used to stress and learn how to deal with it
-It Helps me be confident and less anxious




It's benefits

-Reduces inflammation.
-Helps me Fight my anxiety.
-Increase confidence.
-makes me energetic.



Health Benefits

-Increases endorphins
-Helps improve metabolism
-Improves circulation
-reduce inflammation



My goal

Today is 14 july 2020, My goal is take cold Showers daily for a year staring today, It's summer so i am disappointed because it's going to be easy these days.
