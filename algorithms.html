What are algorithms ? simply

-Algorithms are simply a set of steps needed to acheive a goal or do a task.
-A pizza recipe is an algorithm, the road you take from school to your home is an algorithm.
- It's similar in computer science, Algorithms in comp sci is the SET OF STEPS a program need to do to accomplish a certain task.
-Algorithms are everywhere and used by everybody.
