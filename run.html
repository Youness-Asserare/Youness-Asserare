Running :
One of the cheapest and easiest ways to live a very healthy lifestyle.

Preview

You know that feeling when you go up the stairs and you suddenly find yourself breathing heavy ? Or do you ever feel tired even though you did nothing the whole day ?, Well that's two of many situation where i wish i was more fit. After years of no physical actvities due to depression and other stuff,My cardio game became trash and i felt lazy most of the Days until recently when i got into watching the UFC and started Running/working out, after few days of running, the stairs problem vanished, literaly just few days.



WHY AM I DOING IT ?

-Because it's mentally challenging which means mental toughness.
-To be healthy inside and out
-Doing things that suck is good .
-To not feel tired as much as i do these days
-To up my cardio game




It's benefits

-reduced risk of death from heart attack or stroke
-reduced risk of cardiovascular disease
-lower risk of developing cancer
-lower risk of developing neurological diseases like Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s diseases
